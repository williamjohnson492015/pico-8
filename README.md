Knights of Power - The Last Light
You are a knight with the power of light; however, the deity that bestowed you with this light has fallen. 
The light within you is all that remains as the legions of darkness begin their march on the land. MP is
limited and will not be restored. When it's gone, it's gone. As a knight of the last light, your goal is
to defeat the knight of darkness that leads the local monsters in conquest. Is this a pointless gesture?
Perhaps. But as long as the knight of light draws breath, they'll fight on. The light still burns within.
