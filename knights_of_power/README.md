## 📖 Story

What power you have left is limited and will not be restored. When it's gone, it's gone. As a knight of the 
last light, your goal is to defeat the knight of darkness that leads the local monsters in conquest. 
Is this a pointless gesture? Perhaps. But as long as the knight of light draws breath, they'll fight on. 
The light still burns within.
