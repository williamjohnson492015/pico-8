## 📖 Story

A hero has arrived and your people will likely be the first to be dealt with as the hero grows
their power. The other monsters refuse to unite or come to your aid. The only option left is...
to fight! Use the power of gold coins to come up with different combinations of traits and skills 
to defeat other monsters for their gold coins and ultimately save your people from the hero.

---

## ⚔️ Features

- [ ] Seemless field traversal map
- [ ] Turn-based combat
- [ ] Inventory system
- [ ] Character growth by spending gold for stats, skills, or magic
- [ ] NPCs, dialogue, & choice driven
- [ ] Save system
- [ ] Unlock new titles hidden in the world for more power
- [ ] Boss fights with two optional for extra challenge
- [ ] Limited resources adds a level of strategy
